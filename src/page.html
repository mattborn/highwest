About High West

The land teaches patience. It reminds us that every good thing — every tree, every home, every community — takes time to grow. High West Group builds with that same respect.

We see development as stewardship. To shape a place is to listen first: to its history, its people, and its future. Our work in hospitality and housing begins with that listening.

Each project is an act of care — balancing beauty and utility, permanence and belonging. We believe real estate should add value not only to investors, but to the lives lived within it.

In the mountain West, where nature sets the pace, we build thoughtfully — creating places that feel honest, enduring, and at home in their landscape.

Kevin Perry
General Partner
